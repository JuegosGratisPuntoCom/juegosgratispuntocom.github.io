Juegos Gratis Punto Com (Free Games Dot Com) is a fork of games-site.github.io made by PepeBigotes.

The goal of this fork is to:

Improve the front-end and back-end, Add more games, Remove all tracking and ad features.

I made this for a school project, so I may or may-not abandon development for this fork depending on my situation later.
